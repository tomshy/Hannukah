Mentoring Mate

Personal Project

Year

  • 2017

Link

  • https://mentoringmate.com

Category

  • Product Development

I recently started working with Code Institute as a mentor for their online students.

It quicky became clear that tracking 30+ students and their progress was a challenge. To deal with this, I built a small application to help.

Mentoring Mate allows you to track individual students and add feedback on individual sessions. This makes it much easier to see student progress as well as track the time you’ve spent with each student.
